How We Work with Garages

Our process ensures your car is in good hands from start to finish.

1

Book a Service

Easily book a service online or through our app. Choose a time that works for you, and we'll handle the rest.

2

Car Pickup

We'll pick up your car from your chosen location and bring it to our trusted network of garages for servicing.

3

Service & Check-Up

Our experienced mechanics will service your car, performing all the necessary checks and maintenance.

4

Car Delivery

Once the service is complete, we'll deliver your car back to you, ensuring your satisfaction with the work done.

What We Do

We act as the bridge that connects vehicle owners with a network of trusted service providers, including mechanics, fuel stations, towing services, insurance companies, & car accessory suppliers. Through our mobile app and web platform, we bring convenience & transparency to the automotive industry, ensuring secure cashless transactions & efficient access to a wide range of services.

Request a Mechanic

We have built an outstanding network of garages that may be accessed through our app. Our range of garages offer fast, reliable and secure services to our users whenever and wherever needed.
